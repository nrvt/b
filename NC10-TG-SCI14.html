What do you mean by fuel?
The materials which are burnt to produce heat energy are known as fuels.
Eg: Wood, Coal, LPG, Kerosene.
How charcoal is different from wood?
Charcoal is a product of wood, whereas wood is the raw material.
Charcoal is formed by burning wood in absence of oxygen. Charcoal has higher energy density, produces less smoke & ash and is a more compact fuel when compared to wood.
Biogas is also known as gobar gas. Justify.
Bio-gas is a mixture of gases like methane, $\ce{CO2}$, $\ce{H2}$ & hydrogen sulphide generally formed by anaerobic decomposition of cow-dung & water.
As cow-dung in Hindi is called as 'Gobar'; Hence Bio-gas is also known as gobar gas in Rural India.
Name a device which can be used for cooking so as to save fuel.
Solar cooker is one of the devices which can be used for cooking so as to save fuel.
